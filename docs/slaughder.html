The Project

Vlogging Drone

Project Goals

A Stabilized, Lightweight, Arial Unit Generating High Definition Recording, our vlogging drone is designed to be hands off and easy to use. Powered by a 22.2V battery and controlled by a Nvidia Jetson, you can use this drone to record in a variety of enviornments. Try it today.

My Roles and Contributions

We believe in a fluid work environment, without strictly enforced roles. As an electrical and computer engineer, I took on most of the elctrical systems on the drone as well as the software architecture, communication between flight controller and microcontroller, and flight algorithms.
